Ways to sell your soul

Terms of Service Agreements

By far the most accidental of soul sales, terms of service agreements target the average person who is too lazy to read the terms before selling their soul to use whatever software they receive in exchange. Examples include LinkedIn and Instagram with the latter having the ability to sell your photos at one point.

College Loans

As someone who went to an expensive liberal arts college I have experienced firsthand many of the issues with modern colleges. Since 1980 college prices have skyrocketed having increased 1,445%. This coupled with an undergraduate dropout rate of 40% means that roughly half of individuals who take out loans won't even end up with a degree.

Good Old Contracts

The classic way to sell your soul has always been with contracts. Typically with the devil or some eldritch entity on the receiving end of the exchange, its depiction in media is commonplace.
